Inquira

"To build a machine learning system that identifies key factors responsible for poor product sales and provides data-driven insights to improve performance."

Problem Statement

Many businesses launch products but fail to understand why sales remain low. Companies often rely on assumptions instead of data to diagnose poor performance.

There is no simple system that:

  • Detects why a product is underperforming
  • Identifies major contributing factors
  • Provides interpretable insights
  • Suggests actionable improvements

This project aims to build a predictive and explainable system that analyzes product features and determines the root causes of low sales.

LITERATURE REVIEW

Existing approaches include:

  • Simple regression models predicting sales numbers
  • Sentiment analysis on product reviews
  • Price-demand elasticity analysis

Limitations:

  • Most systems only predict sales
  • They do not explain why the product is failing.
  • They lack feature importance interpretation.
  • Small businesses cannot afford enterprise BI tools.
